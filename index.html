Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 15, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the mobile application Walk Up Music (hereinafter referred to as the "Application") for Android devices, which operates as a dugout game-day audio utility tool designed for youth sports.

1. Information Collection and Use

The Application is built entirely on a local utility concept. All team configurations, player names, lineup orders, audio playback clipping markers, and local parameters are processed and saved strictly within a local database on your own device. The Application does not use network synchronization modules to transmit this data to external cloud storage structures or administrative servers.

2. Device Permissions Required

To deliver seamless, one-tap control in a fast-paced dugout environment, the Application requires access to specific device capabilities. You will be explicitly prompted to grant these dependencies:

  • Storage / Media Files Access: Required to browse, load, and trim audio tracks stored locally on your device to isolate your team's walk-up segments.
  • Audio Recording (Microphone): Required only if you utilize local custom voice features to record and review announcer introductions on-device.

3. Personal Data & Third-Party Sharing

We are firmly committed to user data preservation:

  • No user account creation or login profiles are required to use the Application.
  • We do not collect, track, or monitor persistent hardware identifiers, names, phone numbers, or telemetry locations.
  • Because no analytical tracking platforms or external database links exist in the engine, absolutely no information is ever compiled, processed, or shared with third-party data brokers, analytics monitors, or advertisement target networks.

4. Children’s Privacy Protection

The Application is intended to be used by adult coaches, sports volunteers, and team parents coordinating game-day music rules. Because the utility engine features zero network pipelines and logs no user footprints, it operates in strict alignment with global children's privacy standards (including COPPA) and does not knowingly discover or pull data from children under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy timeline as new versions release. Any amendments will be instantly recorded by publishing the modified text structure directly on this hosted URL resource page.
